Twitter Pull measures how many second-degree followers @Sergey_Valiukh has compared to the average Twitter account. This metric gives a better idea of a user’s clout on Twitter than is possible from considering just the number of followers, as it takes into account how influential those followers are. Thus it is possible to have a high Twitter Pull though relatively few followers, if those followers themselves are by and large big shots on Twitter, with great followings of their own. Twitter Pull is expressed as a quotient: e.g., a pull of 3x means three times more second-degree followers than the average Twitter account.
